What is 2keycat

This is an application I developed called 2Keycat, which helps people subsidize their trips by offering to transport bags on behalf of others. The name "2Keycat" is derived from the Wolof word for traveler, which is "tukkikat".

The purpose of this app is to create a platform where users can accept responsibility for transporting bags and earn money. The app operates on a review-based system to ensure trust, and all transactions are recorded on the blockchain using Solidity.

I have implemented server requests to retrieve data, and by manipulating the DOM with click events, I display the received information. The app uses forms to retrieve data and sends it to a server that stores the information in a PostgreSQL database. I utilized Express for handling HTTP requests, CORS for browser compatibility, and Multer for enabling users to upload pictures of the bags.

The app also includes a feature where clicking on a traveler removes them from the list. Every Sunday at 12:00, a new list is generated, and users can sort by city and dates.

Why its useful

  • 2Keycat provides a convenient and efficient solution for travelers looking to earn extra income and individuals seeking affordable and reliable bag transportation services.
  • 2Keycat offers a cost-effective alternative to traditional postal services for bag delivery, and users can subsidize or fully pay for their own travel by accepting bag transportation requests.
