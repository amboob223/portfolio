What is Baba?

Baba is an innovative app designed to assist new fathers in tracking their newborn's well-being. Leveraging technologies like Node.js, Express, and authentication, Baba provides a comprehensive solution for fathers on their journey into parenthood. The name "Baba" is derived from the Yoruba tribe's term for "father," symbolizing the app's focus on paternal care.

Why It's Useful

  • Baba offers fathers a convenient way to track and schedule appropriate feed times for their newborns, ensuring their well-being and growth.
  • The app also allows fathers to maintain a list of doctors' names and contact numbers, making it easy to access healthcare information when needed.
  • Furthermore, Baba enables fathers to monitor their child's financial future by tracking cryptocurrency and stock choices, ensuring financial security.
  • The "Shelf" feature in Baba allows fathers to store important information such as the child's birthdate and pictures, creating a precious digital memory book.
